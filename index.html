About Us

A few words .........

Team Sportivo is the official sports club of Future Institute of Engineering and Management. It is a club that is dedicated to the promotion of sports and fitness among the students of the college. The club organizes various sports events and tournaments throughout the year to encourage students to participate in sports and lead a healthy lifestyle. The club also provides training and coaching to students who are interested in pursuing sports as a career. Team Sportivo aims to create a culture of sportsmanship and teamwork among the students and help them develop their physical and mental fitness through sports.
